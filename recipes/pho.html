Pho

Description

Pho is the popular soup dish from Vietnam. It is really delicious and flavorful. Personally I prefer other Viet soups like Bun Bo but a lot of people like this one and it is easily accessible so I chose it instead. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Beef bones
  • Stewing beef
  • Oxtail
  • Fresh ginger
  • Onion
  • Daikon
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Green cardamom
  • Star anise
  • Coriander seeds
  • Black cardamom
  • Salt
  • Rock sugar
  • Beef stock powder
  • Fish sauce

Steps

  1. Wash the bones.
  2. Boil onion and ginger
  3. Toast the dry ingredients.
  4. Place all spices in soup bag and in soup.
  5. Add salt sugar and beef stock.
  6. Skim off skum and fat
  7. Add the onion, ginger, spice bag, and daikon
  8. Complete the pho!
  9. Go to Pho Song Hai instead for good chicken pho!
