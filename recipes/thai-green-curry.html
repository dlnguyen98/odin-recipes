Thai Green Curry

Description

Thai green curry is a popular thai dish that is great for weeknight dinners. It is easy to make with store bough curry paste and is delicious!

Ingredients

  • Thai basil
  • Bamboo shoots
  • Red bell pepper
  • Green curry paste
  • Makrut lime leaves
  • Full fat coconut milk
  • Fish sauce
  • Palm sugar
  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • Chicken stock, unsalted

Steps

  1. Optional step: Pound green curry paste and Thai basil leaves for an extra vibrant colour.
  2. Get the paste as fine as possible.
  3. Reduce the coconut milk until super thick.
  4. Add the curry paste and saute for a few minutes.
  5. Toss chicken with the curry paste.
  6. Add coconut milk
  7. Add chicken stock
  8. Add makrut lime leaves.
  9. Add fish sauce and palm sugar.
  10. Simmer for 10-15 min until chicken is fork tender.
  11. Add bamboo shoots and cook for a min until curry boils again.
  12. Stir in red bell pepper and turn off the heat
  13. Add thai basil and stir until just wilted
  14. The green curry is now ready to serve with pizza!
